About Sri Bhaskar Nursery

Cultivating Excellence in Greenery

Sri Bhaskar Nursery is a trusted wholesale plant nursery with our main branch in Muramanda, Kadiyam Mandal and other branches in Madiki, Chemudulanka, and Tokada. We provide premium quality indoor and outdoor plants, avenue trees, flowering plants, and professional garden services across India. We help transform homes, villas, resorts, and commercial spaces into beautiful green environments.
